This game has two secret functions.

Retro Mode:
	Makes screen flicker and slows down to give
	it a more retro feel. Good for movie scenes.
	Fear this if you are photosisitive.
	
	To use retro mode, press F9.

Screenshot:
	Takes a picture of the screen and saves it as
	a bitmap file. There are two examples in this
	folder. Warning: Each time you run this program,
	your screenshot numbers will reset and overwrite
	any previous images.
	
	To take a screenshot, press F12.
